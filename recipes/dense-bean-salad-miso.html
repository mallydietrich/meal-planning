Dense Bean Salad: Miso

Instructions

  1. Rinse and drain the beans and add them to a large salad bowl.
  2. Break down the rotisserie chicken and dice into bite sized pieces. Add to the bowl.
  3. Dice the charcuterie meat into small pieces and use your hands to break it apart.
  4. Dice the parmesan cheese and cheddar and add to the bowl.
  5. Slice the cherry tomatoes in half, mince the parsley and the shallot, and chop the olives.
  6. Add them all to the bowl.
  7. In a small container mix the olive oil, red wine vinegar, both mustards, sea salt, honey,
  8. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss until fully combined.
  9. Enjoy on crackers.
