Kale Caesar White Bean Salad

A simple, high-fiber salad with kale, white beans, and a creamy caesar dressing.

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the kale into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Rinse and drain the white beans.
  3. Toss the kale, beans, and parmesan cheese with caesar dressing until well coated.
  4. Serve as a fresh lunch or side.
