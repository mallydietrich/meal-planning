Southwest Dense Bean Salad

This Dense Bean Salad is high-fibre and protein-packed with a zesty lime dressing‚Äîperfect for meal prep, potlucks, or quick lunches!

Instructions

  1. Mix dressing ingredients (olive oil, lime juice, vinegar, mustard, spices, honey, garlic) together and set aside.
  2. Mix all beans and vegetables in a large bowl.
  3. Toss with dressing well to combine.
  4. Serve right away and enjoy, or let sit up to 30 minutes for flavours to meld before enjoying.
