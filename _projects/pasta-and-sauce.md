# Pasta and Sauce

A simple, quick-bake style meal using premade pasta and sauce.

## Instructions

1. Cook the tortellini according to package instructions.
2. Heat the pasta sauce in a separate pan or in the microwave.
3. Toss the cooked pasta with the sauce.
4. Top with grated parmesan cheese and serve.
