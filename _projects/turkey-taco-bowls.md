# Turkey Taco Bowls



## Instructions

1. **Roast the sweet potato** Peel and dice the sweet potato into 1/2-inch cubes. Toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 400Â°F for 20-25 minutes until tender.
2. **Cook the turkey** Brown the ground turkey in a skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it into crumbles.
3. **Season the turkey** Add the taco seasoning packet with water per package directions. Simmer until thickened.
4. **Prepare the beans** Drain and rinse the black beans.
5. **Prepare the toppings** Dice the tomatoes and white onion. Chop the cilantro.
6. **Assemble bowls** Sweet potato base, seasoned turkey, black beans, tomatoes, onion, cheese, avocado, cilantro.
7. **Finish** Top with taco sauce and a squeeze of lime juice.
