# TJ's Dumpling Bake



## Instructions

1. Combine coconut milk, soyaki, red curry sauce, and garlic. Mix until well blended.
2. Add frozen potstickers / dumplings
3. Add spinach
4. Cover with foil & bake at 400 degrees for 35 minutes
